## What is n8n?

n8n is a free and open-source workflow automation tool that allows you to connect different services and automate tasks. With n8n, you can create powerful workflows without writing code, or extend them with custom JavaScript when needed.

## Popular Use Cases

- **Data synchronization** - Keep your databases, CRMs, and tools in sync
- **Notifications** - Send alerts via email, Slack, Discord, and more
- **Content management** - Automate publishing and content workflows
- **E-commerce** - Process orders, manage inventory, and handle customer data
- **Lead generation** - Capture, qualify, and route leads automatically
- **Social media** - Schedule posts, monitor mentions, and engage with audiences
